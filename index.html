About Me

Scholar. Researcher. Educator.

I am a post-graduate researcher in Information and Communication Engineering at Bangladesh University of Professionals, where I hold the 1st merit position with a perfect CGPA of 4.00.

My research sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare — specifically applying deep learning, multimodal fusion, and explainable AI to clinical image analysis. My M.Sc thesis focuses on Cervical Cancer Detection Using Multimodal Deep Learning, combining Pap smear image analysis with clinical metadata to build interpretable diagnostic tools.

Beyond research, I am deeply committed to teaching and mentorship. As a Teaching Assistant at BUP, I have supported lab facilitation, student counselling, manuscript peer review, and academic administration — preparing me for a full-time lectureship in Computer Science and Engineering.
